Our Home

We have recently renovated our 'Play area' for our Littles. In order to accommodate for all of our Littles, we've installed premium artificial turf. Regardless of allergies, any child can safely participate with outdoor activities such as sports, bubbles, gentle water activities - all while eliminating the chance of grass stains!

Our 'Learning area' has been strategically assembled to allow our littles to use their imaginations and explore. We have plenty of books for designated story time, interactive learning games, space for arts and crafts, activity mats, and a healthy dose of screen time with Leap Frog!

Our 'Quiet room' is designed for our littles to recharge from the excitement they endure here at care. 'Quiet room' is equipped with white noise maker, video/audio baby monitors and separated sleeping areas for personal sleep space.

About Me

I am Bethany Matthews, co-founder of Chae & Marie Solutions, LLC. As a mother, wife, and daughter, I am intimately aware of the anxieties that first-time mothers experience, particularly when returning from maternity leave. My husband and I searched tirelessly for child care options that would provide peace of mind, but every option proved either unrealistic or prohibitively expensive. Ultimately, I decided to leave my career in dentistry behind and devote my time to our future: our children. My goal is to offer mothers and fathers the reassurance that their precious little ones are safe, engaged, and excited to arrive at our care facility each day. By providing a nurturing environment that encourages social interaction and exploration, we aim to teach our children vital social skills in today's socially distant world. Our hope is to restore some normalcy to their lives while fostering a love of learning and growth. "If it takes a village to raise a child, it takes a village to support that child's parent." Ann Douglas
